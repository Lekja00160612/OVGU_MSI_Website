## Tuition Fees
The tuition fee for the Master in Materials Science and Innovation (MSI) program is structured to be competitive while maintaining the highest quality of education, German academic standards, and direct laboratory access.

- **Competitive Structure:** Fees can be paid in convenient installments per semester.
- Please refer to the official VGU website or contact our academic coordinator for the most up-to-date fee schedule for the upcoming academic intake.

## Scholarship Opportunities
We are committed to supporting talented students and ensuring financial constraints do not stand in the way of academic excellence. Several generous scholarship options are available:

### VGU Merit Scholarships
Awarded automatically to candidates with outstanding academic records in their Bachelor's degree. These scholarships can cover up to **100% of the tuition fee**.

### DAAD Scholarships (German Academic Exchange Service)
Through our partnership with OVGU and the German Academic Exchange Service (DAAD), highly qualified students can apply for prestigious DAAD scholarships.
- Covers full tuition fees.
- Provides a monthly living stipend.
- Includes funding and travel allowance for a research stay in Germany at Otto von Guericke University Magdeburg (OVGU).

### Industry Sponsorships
Selected students may receive sponsorships from international industry partners in exchange for conducting their Master thesis on a company-specific project.
