# Nanocrystalline Materials Seminar

Prof. Dr. Elena Vasquez from TU Dresden presented cutting-edge research on severe plastic deformation routes for producing bulk nanocrystalline copper and aluminum alloys, including Hall-Petch breakdown phenomena.
