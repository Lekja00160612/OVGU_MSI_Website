    ## Field Trips and Industry Visits
    We believe in connecting theory to practice. Our students regularly visit leading manufacturing plants, materials testing facilities, and high-tech companies in Vietnam and the region.

    - **Bosch Vietnam:** Exploring advanced automotive components manufacturing.
    - **Intel Products Vietnam:** Insights into semiconductor packaging and testing.
    - **Vina Kraft Paper:** Understanding sustainable materials processing.

    ## Laboratory Sessions
    Hands-on experience is a core component of the MSI program. Students spend significant time in our advanced laboratories:
    
    - **Microscopy Labs:** Operating SEM (Scanning Electron Microscopes) and AFM (Atomic Force Microscopes).
    - **Mechanical Testing:** Performing tensile, fatigue, and hardness tests.
    - **Chemical Synthesis:** Creating novel polymers and nanomaterials.

    ## Research Seminars and Workshops
    Regular seminars featuring guest speakers from OVGU, other international universities, and industry experts. Students also have opportunities to present their own research findings.
