This course provides a comprehensive overview of metals, ceramics, and polymers designed for contact with biological systems. It covers biodegradation kinetics, cell-material interactions, and bio-compatible surface functionalization techniques.

### Key Topics Covered:
- Metallic implants (Titanium alloys, Cobalt-Chrome, Nitinol)
- Bio-ceramics and bioactive glasses (Hydroxyapatite, bioglass)
- Biodegradable polymers (PLA, PGA, hydrogels)
- Scaffolding design and 3D bioprinting for tissue regeneration
