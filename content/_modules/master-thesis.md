The independent, original Master Research Thesis is the flagship capstone of your study. Worth 30 CP, it is compulsory and typically conducted in the 4th semester under joint supervision of OVGU Magdeburg and VGU faculty. Students can work on their thesis at partner institutes in Germany or locally in collaboration with leading industrial sponsors.
