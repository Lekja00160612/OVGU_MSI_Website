Taught in cooperation with visiting computer science and engineering faculty, this course guides students through molecular dynamics (MD), density functional theory (DFT) foundations, and finite element modeling (FEM) code developments.

### Key Topics Covered:
- Crystal lattice vibrations and MD algorithms
- Quantum mechanical modeling of electron densities (DFT)
- Microstructure evolution simulation (phase field modeling)
- Finite element analysis of structural components under mechanical load
