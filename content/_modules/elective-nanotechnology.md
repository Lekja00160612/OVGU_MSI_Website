This module focuses on the synthesis, self-assembly, and characterization of nanomaterials. Students explore the physics of low-dimensional systems and their applications in semiconductor devices, drug delivery systems, and advanced composite reinforcements.

### Key Topics Covered:
- Size effects on electronic and optical properties
- Carbon-based nanomaterials (graphene, carbon nanotubes)
- Synthesis methods (sol-gel, chemical vapor deposition, ball milling)
- Characterization using AFM, TEM, and DLS
