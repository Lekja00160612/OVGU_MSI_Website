## State-of-the-Art Facilities
VGU boasts some of the most advanced materials science laboratories in Southeast Asia. Our students have direct access to equipment that is typically restricted to PhD researchers or industry professionals.

### Materials Characterization Lab
- **Scanning Electron Microscope (SEM) with EDX:** High-resolution imaging and elemental microanalysis.
- **X-Ray Diffractometer (XRD):** Identification and phase analysis of crystalline materials.
- **Atomic Force Microscope (AFM):** Nanometer-scale surface topography and properties.
- **Fourier Transform Infrared Spectrometer (FTIR):** Chemical bond and molecular structure identification.

### Mechanical Testing Lab
- **Universal Testing Machines:** Standard tensile, compression, and bend testing.
- **Micro-hardness Testers:** Vickers and Knoop hardness evaluation.
- **Impact Testing Equipment:** Charpy impact energy measurement.

### Processing and Synthesis
- High-temperature furnaces and sintering systems.
- Polymer extruders and injection molding machines.
- Chemical synthesis hoods and reactors.

## Ongoing Research Highlights
Our faculty and students are actively engaged in research that addresses global challenges.

- **Sustainable Polymers:** Developing biodegradable alternatives to common plastics.
- **Advanced Battery Materials:** Synthesizing novel nanomaterials for higher-capacity lithium-ion batteries.
- **Lightweight Alloys:** Optimizing aluminum and magnesium alloys for the aerospace and automotive sectors.
