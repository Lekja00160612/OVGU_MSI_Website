The SEM is configured with Energy Dispersive X-Ray Spectroscopy (EDX) which enables point-by-point chemical analysis of specimens. Students use it to investigate grain boundaries, phase distributions, fractures, and surface structures of metallic, ceramic, and polymeric materials.
