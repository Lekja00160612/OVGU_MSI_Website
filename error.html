About the Program

Bridging Two Nations, One Degree

The Master in Materials Science (MSI) is a joint academic achievement between Vietnamese-German University (VGU) and Otto von Guericke University Magdeburg (OVGU) — one of Germany's foremost technical universities.

Graduates receive a German-accredited M.Sc. degree, opening doors to careers across Europe, Asia, and beyond in industries from automotive and aerospace to medical devices and sustainable energy.

  • ✓ 4 semesters (2 years) full-time program
  • ✓ English-language instruction
  • ✓ German professors teach in Vietnam
  • ✓ Mandatory research thesis in semester 4
